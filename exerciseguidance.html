Exercise Guidance

Proper form and technique guide for different muscle groups

Chest

  • Bench Press
    4 sets x 12-15 reps | Rest: 90 seconds between sets
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
    3 sets x 12 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  • Push-Ups
    3 sets x 15-20 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Back

  • Lat Pull-Downs
    4 sets x 12 reps | Rest: 90 seconds between sets
  • Seated Cable Rows
    3 sets x 12-15 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  • Deadlifts
    4 sets x 8-10 reps | Rest: 120 seconds between sets

Biceps

  • Barbell Curls
    3 sets x 12 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  • Hammer Curls
    3 sets x 12 reps each arm | Rest: 45 seconds between sets
  • Preacher Curls
    3 sets x 12-15 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Triceps

  • Tricep Pushdowns
    4 sets x 12-15 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  • Skull Crushers
    3 sets x 12 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  • Diamond Push-Ups
    3 sets x 12-15 reps | Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Legs

  • Squats
    4 sets x 12 reps | Rest: 90 seconds between sets
  • Leg Press
    4 sets x 12-15 reps | Rest: 90 seconds between sets
  • Calf Raises
    3 sets x 20 reps | Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Shoulders

  • Military Press
    4 sets x 10-12 reps | Rest: 90 seconds between sets
  • Lateral Raises
    3 sets x 12-15 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  • Front Raises
    3 sets x 12 reps | Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Abs

  • Crunches
    3 sets x 15-20 reps | Rest: 45 seconds between sets
  • Plank
    3 sets x 30-60 seconds | Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  • Russian Twists
    3 sets x 20 reps | Rest: 45 seconds between sets
